Sichuan Boiled Beef

Decription

Learn how to make hot and numbing Sichuan style water boiled beef (Shui Zhu Niu Rou) at home. Sichuan style water boiled beef is one of my favorite dishes in restaurants. If you ever tasted it in China, you will crazily love the tenderness of the beef slices and shocked by how well they are flavored in the hot and red broth.

ingredients

  • beef
  • dried chillies
  • Sichuan pepper
  • Romaine lettuce
  • chilli bean paste

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef: Combine beef slices with rice wine, soy sauce, egg, cornstarch and water. Coat with a thin layer of sesame oil.
  2. Fry the spicy topping: Fry dried chillies and Sichuan peppercorns with a little oil then chop small.
  3. Fry the vegetables: Fry crispy vegetables until they lightly wilt.
  4. Prepare the broth: Fry ginger, garlic, Sichuan chilli bean paste and chilli powder until fragrant. Then pour in stock/water.
  5. Cook the beef: Add marinated beef piece by piece. Simmer until fully cooked. Add to the vegetables.
  6. Sizzle & garnish: Place garlic & chopped spicy topping over the beef. Pour hot oil over. Garnish with coriander.
